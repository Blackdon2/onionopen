July 1st, 2017 Onion Open

Widder Station is a Robert Heaslip design, 18 hole facility that can play from 5300 yards to over 6300 yards. It is a picturesque par 72 course that offers a challenge to most players. We cater to all handicaps with green fees which will please your budget.
